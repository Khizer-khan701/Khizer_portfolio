WorldQuant Data Science Lab Projects

In the WorldQuant Data Science Lab, I am working on a series of real-world data projects that involve extensive data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and predictive modeling using advanced statistical techniques.

These projects cover diverse domains such as real estate (Housing in Mexico & Buenos Aires), environmental science (Air Quality in Nairobi), and disaster management (Earthquake Damage in Nepal).

Lab Highlights

Housing Analysis

Predicting house prices in Mexico and Buenos Aires using regression models.

Air Quality Monitoring

Time series forecasting of air quality in Nairobi using ARIMA models.

Database Management

Handling large datasets using MySQL and MongoDB for efficient retrieval.

Statistical Modeling

Applying decision trees, random forests, and advanced statistical tests for data insights.
