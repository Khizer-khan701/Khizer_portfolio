Steam Minds Project: AI Modules Integration

In the Steam Minds Project, I developed and integrated four core AI modules designed to enhance the learning experience for students: a Chatbot, AI Tutor, Recommendation System, and Quiz Generator.

Leveraging Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and the OpenAI API, these modules provide intelligent, context-aware responses and personalized content to help students navigate their studies more effectively.

Key AI Modules

RAG Chatbot

Steam Minds website chatbot that answers user queries using RAG for high accuracy.

AI Tutor

Assists students during their study sessions with real-time explanations and support.

Recommendation System

Recommends courses to students based on their previous search history and interests.

Quiz Generator

Automatically generates quizzes based on course content using the OpenAI API.

The modules were implemented using Python, Langchain, and RAG techniques to ensure the responses are grounded in provided data. This integration has significantly improved student engagement and personalized learning paths.
